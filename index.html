Our Mountain Venue in the heart of Korea.

A breath-taking venue just a short distance from Daejeon, the mountains of Daejeon are among the most romantic and magical venues in Korea. With acres of tranquil grounds, beautiful places, Korean countryside, and impeccable facilities, our city is located in the heart of Korea and makes for the most perfectly unique day.

Daejeon can host every part of your fairytale visit. From arrival to the leaving and every moment in between, let us help you create a day of memories you and your companions will treasure for a lifetime.

The beauty of our venue can only be seen to be completely believed. From the forests to the lakes, book a visit and view Daejeon for yourself.
