Gye-Jok-San
'Chicken foot' Mountain

Gyejoksan is a mountain of South Korea. It has an elevation of 429 meters. The mountains are spread like chicken feet. In the west, Sungjae Mountain (390 m) stands side by side. It is made up of mountain peaks in four round circles, followed by mountains by Kwangbyeongsan, Usanbong, and Gahaksan. The mountain is the location of Jangdong Green Bath which opened in June 1995. Gyejoksan is one of the eight popular places for sightseers which are designated by city government. Those eight sights include the mountains Sikjangsan, Bomunsan, Gubongsan (구봉산), and Jangtaesan, the lake Daecheonghosu, as well as Yuseong Spa, and Expo Science Park.
